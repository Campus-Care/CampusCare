Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is CampusCare and who can use it?
CampusCare is an initiative designed to help IIT Roorkee students report and track campus-related issues such as hostel maintenance, mess food quality, infrastructure, and more. It is exclusively for students, faculty, and authorized staff of IITR.

Q2. Can we send email to multiple people at the same time?
Once you submit the issue, you will be redirected to your gmail where you can add other people to send the email too.
