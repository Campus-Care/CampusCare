How To Use

Step 1: From the homepage, select the section that matches your concern.

Step 2: On the selected page, choose the specific issue you are facing from the dropdown menu.

Step 3: Then fill in your Name, Enrollment Number, and Room Number and then submit the form.

Step 4: You will be redirected to Gmail with your complaint written in formal manner.
