About Us

CampusCare is an initiative by IIT Roorkee students to simplify and modernize the way campus-related issues are reported and resolved. Our goal is to empower every student to raise their voice and bring positive change within the campus environment.

By using intelligent automation, CampusCare identifies your issue, drafts a professional email, and connects you directly with the concerned authority. It’s your voice, amplified — making communication faster, easier, and more effective.
